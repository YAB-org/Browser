YAB Browser
YAB is a modern browser designed for the buss:// protocol by WebX+. Lightweight yet powerful, YAB offers a sleek and stable environment for exploring the buss web and now even third party protocols!

With full support for the latest WebX+ features, YAB makes experimentation easy and enjoyable. Each tab runs in its own dedicated process, ensuring that a misbehaving site won’t crash your entire browser—just close the tab directly from the taskmanager and keep going.

YAB also includes an extended, browser-specific API that builds on the core WebX+ system, enabling developers to push the boundaries of what’s possible on this new web.
